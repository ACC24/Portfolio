About Me

My name is Andreia Chapman. I'm 39 years old and I was born in Brazil. I moved to the US 13 years ago after meeting my husband at work.
I graduated from college in 2013 with a B.A. in Psychology and I'm currently enrolled in the master's program in Clinical Mental Counseling at the University of West Geogia.
My ultimate goal is to get a PhD in either Counseling or Psychology and become a university professor.
At this moment I'm also enrolled in Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp as a second career option. I have 3 children, 2 cats, a dog and over 20 chickens.
